##basic design outline

1. introduction
    a. show hometown
    b. introduce villagers
    c. introduce hero(es)
    
2. conflict
    a. hometown's conflict explained
    b. introduce enemy/nemesis/issue
    c. prepping to leave hometown
    
3. main
    a. leveling/questing
    b. gear
    c. combat
    d. finding a way to defeat enemy/nemesis/issue
    
4. climax & conflict resolution
    a. boss fight
    b. twist reveal*
    c. final boss fight*

5. resolution/wrap up
    a. return to hometown
    b. wrap up story
    c. what happens to everyone
    d. set up sequal
